Official Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Welcome to Samvidha Hub. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data within the application.

1. Information We Collect

Samvidha Hub is an institutional student portal. We collect and process the following data to provide educational services:

  • Personal Information: Name, Institutional Email Address, and Roll Number (User ID).
  • Academic Data: Attendance records, marks, results, and timetables.
  • Uploaded Content: PDF documents (Lab records/Recordings) uploaded by the user.
  • App Performance: Device type, crash logs, and login timestamps for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate secure login and account management.
  • To display academic progress and schedules to the student.
  • To allow submission of lab records to institutional servers.
  • To monitor app performance and fix technical bugs.

3. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL/TLS encryption, to protect your data during transit. Academic data and files are stored securely on cloud infrastructure (Firebase/AWS) and institutional servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes Google Play Services and Firebase for core functionality. These providers may collect information used to identify your device in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. No Account Creation

Please note that Samvidha Hub does not allow users to register or create accounts. All accounts are pre-issued and managed by the educational institution. If you wish to have your data modified or restricted, please contact your college administrator.
